📘 OSINT Navigator Guide

🔹 Step 1: Enter any IP address, domain, URL, or hash into the search bar.

➕ Step 2: Click the ADD button. This will:

  • Automatically append your input to each relevant OSINT tool’s URL.
  • Update all tool cards with active links based on your query.

⚡ Step 3: Click any tool to open it — your search input is already preloaded!

💡 Bonus Tips:

  • You can reorder cards by dragging and dropping them within or between categories.
  • Changes are saved automatically in your browser (locally).
  • Use the filter bar to quickly find tools by name or keyword (e.g., "dns", "threat").
  • Double-click the filter to reset it.
  • Tools marked with 🔶 work standalone and do not reflect search queries.

🧼 To reset layout: Open console and run:

localStorage.removeItem("osintToolOrder");
